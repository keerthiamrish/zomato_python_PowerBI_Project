# 🍽️ Zomato Data Analysis & Restaurant Insights

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Zomato restaurant data to uncover insights related to customer preferences, restaurant performance, pricing strategies, and delivery trends. The analysis helps understand how different factors like location, cuisine, and cost influence ratings and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze restaurant ratings and identify high-performing restaurants
- Study the relationship between price range and customer ratings
- Evaluate delivery performance across different locations
- Identify the most popular cuisines among customers
- Detect trends and patterns to support business decision-making

---

## ⚙️ Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- Handled missing values in ratings and cost columns
- Removed duplicates and inconsistent entries
- Converted data types for accurate analysis
- Filtered irrelevant or incorrect data

---
## 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Distribution of restaurant ratings
- Analysis of price range vs ratings
- Top-performing locations based on ratings
- Cuisine-wise popularity analysis
- Delivery rating comparison across restaurants

---
## 📊 Dashboard & Visualization
- Created interactive dashboards using Power BI
- Used charts such as:
  - Bar charts (Top cuisines, locations)
  - Pie charts (Delivery vs Non-delivery)
  - Line charts (Trend analysis)
- Visualized key performance metrics for easy understanding

---
## 🔍 Key Insights
- Restaurants with ratings above 4.0 are mostly in mid to high price ranges
- Certain locations consistently show higher restaurant ratings
- Popular cuisines like North Indian and Chinese dominate the market
- Delivery ratings vary significantly and impact customer satisfaction
- Restaurants offering online delivery tend to attract more customers

---
## 🧱 Technologies Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy for data analysis)
- Matplotlib / Seaborn (for visualization)
- Power BI (for dashboard creation)

---

---
## 📌 Business Recommendations
- Focus on maintaining high ratings (above 4.0) to attract customers
- Improve delivery service in low-performing areas
- Invest more in popular cuisines to increase revenue
- Optimize pricing strategies for better customer engagement
